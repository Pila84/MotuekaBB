Privacy Statement

  1. Collection of Personal Information

    We collect personal information from you, which includes:

    • Name: This includes your first and last name. Collecting this information allows us to personalize your experience and identify you for bookings and reservations.
    • Email Address: Your email is used for communication purposes, such as sending booking confirmations and updates about our services.
    • Preferences (e.g., room preferences, dietary requirements)
    • Password: For registered users, we require a password to create and secure your account on our website.

  2. Purpose of Collecting Your Information

    The primary purpose for collecting this information is to provide and manage our services effectively, including processing your bookings and personalizing your experience with us.

  3. Sharing of Your Information

    We may share this information with third-party service providers to the extent necessary for providing our services. These parties are obligated to use your information solely for the purposes of assisting us in these services.

  4. Optional Information

    Providing some information is optional. However, if you choose not to enter your personal information, we might be unable to provide certain services to you.

  5. Duration of Storage

    Your personal information is kept only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.

  6. Security and Access

    We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted files and restrict access to management staff only.

    You have the right to request access to, or correction of, any personal information we hold about you. To do so, please contact us at info@motuekab&b.co.nz
